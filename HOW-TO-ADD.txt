VIDYADHAN CLASSES — REAL Contact mobile fix (right side was cut off)
===================================================================
Copy into your cloned GitHub folder (overwrite), then Commit -> Push,
wait 1-2 min, then HARD REFRESH (or Incognito) on your phone.

  app/contact/page.tsx   -> overwrite
  app/globals.css        -> overwrite

WHAT WAS WRONG
  On mobile the contact column was wider than the screen, so the right side
  got cut off (address "...400605", the email, the WhatsApp button, the form
  heading). The earlier fix wrapped text visually but didn't shrink its width,
  so it still overflowed.

WHAT THIS FIXES
  - Forces the contact column to fit the phone width (grid-cols-1).
  - Long email/address now truly wrap and shrink (overflow-wrap:anywhere),
    so nothing gets cut off.
  - Map limited to screen width; html+body guard against sideways scroll.
